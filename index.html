FC Barcelona

Barcelona Football Club was founded in November 1899 by a group of players from a quarter of Swiss, English, German and Spanish nationalities led by Spaniard of Swiss origins Joan Gamper, and the club has become a symbol of Catalan culture and Catalan nationalism, and for this its motto is “Més que un club.

Barcelona's official anthem is "Nobody Can Conquer Us," written by Jaume Bekas and Josep Maria Espinas , and composed by Manuel Valls. Unlike many other football clubs, the supporters own and operate Barcelona. It is one of the richest football clubs in terms of revenue, with total revenues for the 2014-15 season amounting to 560.8 million euros. The club holds a long-standing rivalry with Real Madrid, and their matches are called traditional matches.


Barcelona is one of the most successful clubs in the history of Spanish football in terms of the number of local tournaments. It has won twenty-six league titles. a record number of thirty-one Spanish Cup titles, thirteen Spanish Super Cups and two League Cups. It is also one of the most successful clubs in the history of European football at the level of European championships, as it achieved 17 European continental titles by winning the European Champions League five times, 4 times the European Cup Winners’ Cup, 5 times the European Super Cup, in addition to winning the Club World Cup. three times
